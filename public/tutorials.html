1.Banking Explained [English]:

Banking refers to the business of accepting deposits, providing loans, and other financial services to individuals, businesses, and governments. Banks play a vital role in facilitating economic transactions, managing financial risks, and supporting economic growth.


2.How Savings Account Work [English]:

A savings bank is a type of financial institution that specializes in accepting and safeguarding savings deposits. Unlike commercial banks, which offer a wide range of financial services, savings banks primarily focus on providing a secure place for individuals to deposit their savings while earning some interest.
